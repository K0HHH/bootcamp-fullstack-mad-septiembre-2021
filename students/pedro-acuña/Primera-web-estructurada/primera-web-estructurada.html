Ruta 66

U.S. Route 66 or U.S. Highway 66 (US 66 or Route 66), also known as the Will Rogers Highway, the Main Street of America or the Mother Road, was one of the original highways in the U.S. Highway System. US 66 was established on November 11, 1926, with road signs erected the following year.

Rock It Dinner

Wall mural Rock It Diner Cafe. Image of the classic cafeteria of the 50s, in the height of rock and roll. We see a sunset with the cafeteria in perspective, a large luminous sign to make it look good from the distance of the road and several cars, including a cadillac and a motorcycle.
